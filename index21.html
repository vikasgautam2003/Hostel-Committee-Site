    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is the fees to join the hostel committee?

    The is Rs100/-

    How can students get involved in the activities organized by the Hostel Committee?

    Students can participate by attending meetings, volunteering for events, suggesting ideas, and joining event planning committees.

    Are there any leadership or volunteer opportunities within the Hostel Committee?

    Yes, students can apply for leadership roles or volunteer for event planning and execution to gain valuable experience.

    What support does the university provide to the Hostel Committee in organizing events?

    The university may provide funding, logistical support, and access to facilities, enhancing the committee's capacity to organize successful events.

    What role does the Hostel Committee play in addressing concerns or grievances raised by hostel residents?

    The committee serves as a liaison between residents and university administration, advocating for solutions to improve hostel life.
